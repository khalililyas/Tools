Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service free to use?

Yes, our image compression tool is completely free with no hidden charges or watermarks.

What's the maximum image size I can compress?

You can compress images up to 20MB in size. For best results with large images, we recommend resizing them first.

Will compressing my image reduce its quality?

Some quality reduction is inevitable with lossy compression, but our tool lets you balance between file size and quality to get the perfect result.

What's the difference between JPG, PNG and WebP?

JPG is best for photos, PNG for images with transparency, and WebP offers superior compression with quality similar to JPG but with smaller file sizes.
